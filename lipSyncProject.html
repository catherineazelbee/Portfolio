Character Lipsync

Animation by Catherine Azelby

Made using Autodesk Maya 2025

Character Model/Rig by Francois Boquet

Audio from DanDaDan English Dub

Project Description

The goal of this project was to learn the fundamentals of lip sync for 3D animation. I used audio from the English dub of DanDaDan, as I really lvoe the voice acting for the character Momo! I also utilized the Waitress character model from Francois Boquet, as I thought the audio would be fitting for a waitress character, totally exhausted and mad from a shift. I wanted to focus on expressing the flow of her emotions, from super frustrated, trying to calm herself down, exploding with anger, and suprise of being interrupted.
